What Is an Education Loan
Eligibility Calculator?

Deciding to opt for an education loan is often the first step in your education finance journey. Now you have to think about the amount of money you’d require and how you can pay the amount back within the loan tenure. An education loan eligibility calculator helps you understand your finances better. The eligibility calculator provides you with the approximate education loan amount you are eligible to borrow based on your current finances. You need to provide details about your monthly income, monthly obligations, the interest rate and estimated tenure of your loan. If you do not have a monthly income, you need to provide your co-borrower’s details instead. Based on the information provided, the eligibility calculator will provide you with an estimate of your education loan eligibility amount.
