What Is Education Loan Eligibility Criteria?

If you’re hoping to secure a loan for your higher studies in India or abroad, we can provide you with an education loan. Before you apply, you need to meet the following eligibility criteria.

Students can apply if they:

  • Are Indian citizens who are 18 or older
  • Have a confirmed admission before the final disbursal
  • Have a co-borrower who earns in India

Co-borrowers also need to meet the following criteria:

  • Must be an Indian citizen and should be a parent, legal guardian, sibling or other blood relative
  • Must have a bank account in India that allows them to sign cheques
  • Must be the primary debtor

You can apply for a loan for graduate or post-graduate courses or other job-oriented professional or technical courses. The courses and the institute offering them must be approved by Avanse.

If you meet the above criteria, you can go ahead and use the education loan calculator to understand your education loan eligibility in India. Your student loan eligibility in India depends on your monthly earnings and outgoings as well as your loan interest and tenure.

Why Use an Education Loan Eligibility Calculator?

As a young adult, understanding your finances can seem difficult. You may require a certain amount to secure your seat at your preferred college, but your financial situation dictates the maximum amount a lender will provide as your education loan. Your student loan eligibility depends on your family’s current earnings and outgoings and your loan tenure and interest rate. Your lender will use this information to understand whether or not you are capable of repaying your loan on time. If you apply for a student loan in excess of your student loan eligibility, your application could get rejected. If this happens, it could dishearten you and affect your credit score in the future.

When you opt to use the education loan eligibility calculator, you will gain a realistic understanding of your financial standing. You can use the information to apply for a loan amount that lenders can afford to provide you based on your education loan eligibility in India. You can use the education loan eligibility calculator to make informed decisions about your higher education and financial future.
