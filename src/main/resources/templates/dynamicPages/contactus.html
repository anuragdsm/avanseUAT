Customer Grievance

Avanse Education Loan Grievance Redressal Mechanism

We have a three-tier system to ensure that you get the help you need.

  • Level 1
  • Level 2
  • Level 3

For service requests or complaints, you can contact our Customer Engagement team at:

wecare@avanse.com OR

1-800-266-0200 if you're in India OR

(+91) 22-6680-6464 if you're abroad OR

Send a written request to Avanse Financial Services Limited, 001 & 002 Fulcrum, A-Wing, Ground Floor, Sahar Road, Next to Hyatt Regency, Andheri (East), Mumbai – 400 099.

Once we receive the request or complaint, we'll share an acknowledgement within two working days. We might reach out over e-mail, a letter in the post, SMS or other electronic communication that is legally valid, including WhatsApp. Our aim is to respond to your query within 10 working days.

If we need to investigate your complaint, we may need more than 10 working days to resolve your issue.

If you aren't happy with our response, you can escalate the issue to Level 2.

If you’re unhappy with the resolution provided in Level 1 or haven't received an adequate response to your query, you can escalate it to our Grievance Redressal Officer (GRO). You can contact the GRO at:
