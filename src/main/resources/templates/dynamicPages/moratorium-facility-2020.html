Moratorium Facility Registration

As a response to COVID19 situation in India, RBI has issued directives permitting lending institutions to grant moratorium on loan instalments between 1st March 2020 and 31st May 2020. Eligible customers may register their request for the moratorium facility with respect to their loan with us, due to the economic fallout of the COVID 19 pandemic.
